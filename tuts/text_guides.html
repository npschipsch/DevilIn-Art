1. How to Draw a Cute Wolf


Step 1:
Let's add the head now. It should be round and about twice as big as the body.
Step 2:
Add some volume to the forelegs and the feet. They should be quite puffed.
Step 3:
Time for the thigh. Make it very round, connecting directly with the foot.
Step 4:
Make the eyes big, but leave a lot of space for the forehead—a huge forehead will make it look younger and cuter.
Step 5:
The ears can be either huge or tiny—both extremes will add to the cute feeling.
Step 6:
Now the tail. If you can draw it all with one shape, go ahead. If not, here's a trick: start with a circle add an oval
Step 7:
... and finish with a triangle.
Step 8:
Take your darker tool now and finish the details. This time you're allowed to press harder, but be careful—these are the final lines! You don't need to copy me—just look at your base and add some wolf features to it. It will look cute no matter what you do!
